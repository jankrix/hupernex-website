Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hupernex ("Hupernex", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our websites and products, including CVGym and other Hupernex micro-SaaS products. Hupernex is an independently run startup studio, built and operated by a small team based in Indonesia; for legal, regulatory, and payment-processing purposes, the studio's operations are registered under Erick Liemarga, who holds the individual proprietorship on the studio's behalf.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register for a Hupernex product;
  • Payment information: billing details processed by our payment partner, Midtrans — Hupernex does not store full payment card numbers;
  • Usage data: information about how you interact with our products, such as pages visited and features used;
  • Content you provide: information you submit to use a product's features (e.g., resume content uploaded to CVGym).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our products;
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions through Midtrans;
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, or support requests;
  • Improve and develop new features across Hupernex products;
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues.

3. Payment Processing

All payments made for Hupernex products are processed by Midtrans, a licensed third-party payment gateway. Midtrans collects and processes payment information in accordance with its own privacy policy. Hupernex does not have access to your full payment card details.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how our products are used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as Midtrans for payments, or hosting providers) solely to operate our products, or when required by law.

6. Data Retention and Security

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide our products and for legitimate business or legal purposes. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email below, subject to applicable law.

8. Children's Privacy

Hupernex products are not directed to individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at contact@hupernex.com.
